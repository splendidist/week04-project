the hobbit

1937

j. r. r. tolkien

In J.R.R. Tolkien's timeless classic, 'The Hobbit,' embark on an epic adventure through the enchanting landscapes of Middle-earth. Bilbo Baggins, a humble hobbit living a quiet life in the Shire, is unexpectedly thrust into a journey of courage, discovery, and self-discovery when the wise wizard Gandalf and a company of thirteen dwarves arrive on his doorstep. Join Bilbo as he ventures far from the comforts of home, encountering trolls, elves, goblins, and a creature of unspeakable darkness known as Smaug the dragon. Along the way, Bilbo discovers inner strengths he never knew he possessed and forms unbreakable bonds of friendship. 'The Hobbit' is a captivating tale of heroism, friendship, and the enduring power of the human spirit. With its richly imagined world, unforgettable characters, and timeless themes of bravery and redemption, this beloved masterpiece continues to enchant readers of all ages and inspire generations.
